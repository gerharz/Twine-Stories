[[Go]]SOLITAIRE II: NOT REALLY SOLITAIRE is a "game" in which you try to assemble a straight, a chronological set of three cards with the same suit. Like: 2 of clubs, 3 of clubs, 4 of clubs Or: J of spades, Q of spades, K of spades To start the game, first draw 3 cards from the deck. Once you've done that, actual gameplay begins. At the start of each of your turns, roll a six-sided die. Then draw X cards from the deck, where X is the number that roll resulted in. From those X cards, you may pick one to put into your hand. The rest go to the bottom of the deck in a random order. Repeat until a straight has been assembled. Double-click this passage to edit it.
